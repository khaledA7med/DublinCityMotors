About Us

Dublin City Motors – Where Style Meets Precision in German High-Spec Cars

At DCM, we trust in German engineering. Our passion for these elegantly powerful machines goes back more than 30 years. What started as a fascination became a full-time profession – buying and selling the favorite cars of automotive enthusiasts worldwide.

Starting out with focus on iconic gems such as the BMW E30, Audi Ur Quattro, and VW Golf GTI from the golden era of the 80s and 90s, we now source nearly new, low-mileage, highly maintained cars for today’s Audi and BMW car enthusiasts.

DCM, founded in 2015, is the culmination of years spent supplying the Irish market with high-spec UK-imported cars. Today, DCM brings its expertise to sourcing cars worldwide, catering to those seeking the epitome of performance, style, and craftsmanship in German automobiles.

As a luxury car boutique, what sets us apart is not merely the stunning cars we offer, but also the personalized experience we provide. At DCM, we understand that each customer is unique and finding their dream vehicle an easy and enjoyable journey is our specialty.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful – to ensure our customers’ utmost satisfaction and foster lasting relationships with them. We take immense pride in being at the forefront of our industry, operating on remarkably low margins without ever compromising on quality or service.

Trust is not bestowed but earned. We believe that your experience will give you the confidence to come back time and time again.
